Diagtech Auto is a company that was established by Henry Mmola, who is a qualified technician since 2007, he had a dream of starting up an auto maintenance company which would offer a better quality services to every client and affordable rates.

We at Diagtech Auto understand that people are always looking for an honest, affordable and clean auto repair facility which is committed to customer services and reliability.
